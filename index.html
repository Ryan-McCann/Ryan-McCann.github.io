About Me

Hello, my name is Ryan McCann. I'll be graduating from Saint Xavier University in May 2026 with a Bachelor's in Computer Science. Many of my projects began as ideas that I wanted to explore or personal projects to fit my needs, which motivates me to see them through to completion. I enjoy working on a variety of projects, ranging from software development to electronics design and 3D CAD. I especially like working on things that require me to learn new skills, solve interesting problems, and develop thoughtful and practical solutions.

Projects

Some of the projects I've worked on include an encoder for arcade controls to USB, a home inventory system for tracking current stock of groceries, an N64 controller to PC interface using a Pi Pico, and a few game prototypes using different libraries and engines.
